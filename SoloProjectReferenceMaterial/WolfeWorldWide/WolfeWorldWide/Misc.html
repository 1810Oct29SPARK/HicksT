About!

Motivations

This website was themed after something im rather passionate about; The art of motorsports. From professional level Nascar Racing to local Drifting and Autocross events, I have experienced it a lot and I am looking to experience more. This Web page was designed with the thought in mind of creating awareness and bringing together Motorsports knowledge on a social level!

I've watched companies like MotorTrends, The Smoking Tire, SpeedHunters, throughout the years on Youtube and other social platforms reviewing all of the latest vehicle technologies day-after-day bringing together fans from various parts of the world through photography, events, comedy, stability, and new technology.
Ive wanted to bring together the culmination of those items creating my own social networking page where people who do not have the company-funded platforms to come together and plan and post:

- local race events
- new racetracks
- updates to their vehicles
- q&a on mechanical issues/ideas (forum style)
- etc!

Thanks for your time
